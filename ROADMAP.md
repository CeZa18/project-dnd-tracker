# ⚔️ D&D Combat Tracker — Project Roadmap

> A personal D&D 5e initiative and combat tracker built for Dungeon Masters.  
> Tracks HP, AC, death saves, conditions, and turn order — all in the browser.

---

**Active Phase:** Phase 2 — Gameplay Enhancements  
**Overall Progress:** ~28% complete  
**Last Updated:** July 2026
